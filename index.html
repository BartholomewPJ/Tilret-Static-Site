Our Process

Initial Consultation

We go through our client questionnaire to understand your goals for the website and discuss what features and support will be most appropriate for those goals.

Proposal

Based on our discussion, we put together a proposal with pricing that outlines the features and services that will and will not be included in the project. We outline what you can expect from us and what we expect from you.

Agreement

Once we are in agreement on the scope of the project, a contract is signed and the subscription period begins. Up-front fees and first month payment is due at that time. You should begin to assemble the website content you plan on sending to us.

Design

It may take up to 5 – 7 days to assemble one or more website mockup designs that we will present to you. The mockup will contain dummy text and placeholder images but should give you an indication of the proposed design.  You sign off on a design you like and we start the actual build of your website. You must send us the bulk of the website content at that time. We can also assist you with registering a domain name and finding a fast and reliable hosting provider.

Build and Revisions

It will take 7 – 14 days to build the website with your content, chosen features, and chosen design. We upload the website build to our development server and present it to you for testing and comments. You will have up to three rounds of revisions so we can build the website exactly as you want it. Each round of revisions may take 3 – 5 days.

Sign-Off and Launch

When you are happy with the build, you sign-off with your approval and we move the website to a live server of a hosting company of your choosing.

Monthly Maintenance and Support

We perform backups, updates, security scans, minor revisions, and other support activities as detailed in your specific service agreement.
